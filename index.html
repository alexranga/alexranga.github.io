Aaaaar!! you ready for a Treasure Quest?

Game Rules

  • There are nine shipwrecks to choose from.
  • In the shipwreck, you could find a map, a treasure, or a pirate.
  • Collect as many maps as you can but beware of the Pirate.
  • You lose all the treasures you've collected if a pirate finds you.
  • A map means keep searching!
  • If you find the treasure, you win!
